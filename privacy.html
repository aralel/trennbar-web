Privacy Policy

This policy explains what Cyrus Cylinder collects, why it is collected, and how users can contact the app operator.

Read Terms Open on Google Play Open on App Store

1. Information We Collect

Cyrus Cylinder may store limited app preferences on the device, such as onboarding status, selected translation language, auto-read preferences, notification settings, and local usage counters for random-word attempts.

If advertising is enabled in the production app, third-party ad providers may collect device and ad interaction data according to their own policies.

2. How We Use Information

  • To remember user settings inside the app.
  • To power notifications when another try becomes available.
  • To improve app stability, performance, and release quality.
  • To support advertising, if ads are enabled in production.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may rely on third-party services such as Apple App Store, Google Play, Expo infrastructure, Google Mobile Ads, and device text-to-speech systems. Those providers may process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

4. Data Storage

Preference data is primarily stored locally on the user device. If server-backed services are added later, this policy should be updated to reflect that change.

5. Children

The app is intended for language learning. If the service is offered to children in regulated regions, review local compliance requirements before release.

6. User Choices

Users can manage notifications and other local preferences from inside the app settings. They can also uninstall the app to remove local app data from the device.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@aralel.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the app, monetization model, or backend services evolve. The latest version should always be published on this website.
